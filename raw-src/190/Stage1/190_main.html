A Preface

The word “Culture” has come into use greatly in recent times. It may be said that the main reason for this is because the word “Culture” in English is being used a lot and has gained currency in modern usage.

The word “Culture” in English in today’s context and meaning is being used since about a hundred years. Its use has attained additional significance especially in the last fifty years, gradually. After the United Nations was born (1945) the use of this word is abundant. In 1946, when the UNESCO was formed, the word “Culture” was incorporated in its very name and gave it an authoritative place. The Indian Government has hoisted the flag of culture.

Then what is the meaning of this word which has such an influential position?

Nobody has yet authoritatively defined the meaning of the word. In parlance, there are many loose meanings. A society of scholars has not brought it to a conclusive stage. If you look at the history of such a society, in its discussion of this question, instead of solving the question, it looks as though a fresh generation has got an argument to contend with.
