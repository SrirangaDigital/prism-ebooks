ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

My study regarding the origins and migration of the Aryans and others, has been a typical case of a pigmy perched on the shoulders of a giant, seeing a little bit more than the giant. The giants in this case have been Lokmanya B.G. Tilak,

P. Sangarapillai, Swami Harshananda of Ramakrishna Math, Bangalore, James Watson Luigi Luca Cavalli -Sforza, Spencer Wells, N.S. Rajaram, David Frawtey and S. Kalyanaraman. What I saw was not only a little bit more than what they saw, but it was at times, interesting, exciting and thought provoking. I acknowledge those giants as my teachers for adding to my knowledge.

As the books authored by B.G. Tilak are almost a century old, I had difficulty to get copies. Fortunately, one important book was available at the Indian Institute of World Culture, but it was too fragile to handle. It is Air Marshall B.U. Chengappa (Retd.) who helped me to get the book from Pune. I must acknowledge his very valuable help.

Dr. Kumarasamy Thangaraj of CCMB, Hyderabad and Dr. Ravi Korisettar of Karnataka University were very helpful with prompt responses to my request for scientific papers. Their help encouraged me to continue with my difficult task I had undertaken. I must acknowledge their valuable contribution.

I have also been benefitted by media reports in The Hindu, Times of India and Daily Thanthi apart from valuable imputs from
