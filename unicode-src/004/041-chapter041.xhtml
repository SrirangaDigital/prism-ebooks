Belief for Life

Life’s enthusiasm, human effort, universal friendliness – progress, science, and humility are the three mantras for life. The classical example for this is well represented by our great poet in form of Hanuman in epic Ramayana. Hanuman aptly symbolizes devotion without selfishness, undaunted human effort and dedication filled with enthusiasm. Let this Hanuman be the Guru and guiding light for all Indians.

Look at Hanuman – Teacher of Courage

Pray for his strength and bravery,

Let his form inspire our mind.
