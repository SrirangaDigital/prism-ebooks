"Akashbhara surjotara Viswabhara pran...."

My songs arise when I wonder how,
in this vast universe studded with suns and stars,
and in this world full of life,
even I could have a place!

My songs arise when I wonder that
the waves from eternity that swing
the ebb and tide of this earth,
also throb in my veins!

My songs arise as I step on the grass
on my way to the woods,
startled by the fragrance of flowers,
feel overwhelmed to see all the gifts of joy spread around,

My songs arise when I stretch my ears,
look around with my eyes wide open,
pour out my heart on this earth,
and look for the Unknown in the known.

Rabindranath Tagore

(Translated by the author)
