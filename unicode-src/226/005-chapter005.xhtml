Class Prayer

Om Aapada MouLi Paryantam
GuruNaam Aakritim Smaret
Tena Vignaaha Pranashyanthi
Sidhyanti cha Manorataaha

ॐ आपादमौलिपर्यन्तं गुरूणाम् आकृतिं स्मरेत् ।
तेन विघ्नाः प्रणश्यन्ति सिद्ध्यन्ति च मनोरथाः ।।

Om, to overcome the difficulties in life, meditate on the form of a Guru. Through His Grace, you will overcome all the hurdles and will achieve your goal.

Yogena Chittasya Padena Vachaam
malam Sharirasya cha Vaidyakena
Yopakarottam Pravaram Muninaam
Patanjalim Praanjaliraanatosmi

योगेन चित्तस्य पदेन वाचां मलं शरीरस्य च वैद्यकेन।
योऽपाकरोत् तं प्रवरं मुनीनां पतञ्जलिं प्राञ्जलिरानतोऽस्मि ।।

Through Yoga mind becomes clear, through grammar speech becomes clear, through Ayurveda bodily impurities are removed. Our prostrations to Patanjali Rishi who is the authority in all these three areas.

Ananta Gunapoornaya
Doshadooraya Vishnave
Namaha Shri Prananaathaya
Bhakta aBeesta Pradayine

अनन्तगुणपूर्णाय दोषदूराय विष्णवे ।
नमः श्रीप्राणनाथाय भक्ताभीष्टप्रदायिने ।।

Prostrations to the Lord Vishnu, who has the infinite auspicious qualities and who is beyond defects and is the Lord of Lakshmi, who fulfils desires of all devotees.

Buddir Balam Yasho Dhairyam
Nirbhayatvam Arogatha
Ajadyam Vakpatutwam cha
Hanumat Smaranat Bhavet

बुद्धिर्बलं यशो धैर्यं निर्भयत्वमरोगता ।
अजाड्यं वाक्पटुत्वं च हनूमत्स्मरणाद् भवेत् ।।

By contemplating on Hanuman, one gets all eight siddhis -- intelligence, strength, glory, courage, fearlessness, good health, vigour (energy) and mastery of speech.

Satchidananda Roopaya
Vishvotpatyadhi Hetave
Tapatraya Vinashaya
Sri Krishnaya Namo Namaha

सच्चिदानन्दरूपाय विश्वोत्पत्त्यादिहेतवे ।
तापत्रयविनाशाय श्रीकृष्णाय नमो नमः ।।

Our prostration to the Lord Krishna, who is the embodiment of Satchidananda, Lord of the Universe, the remover of all three taapas (adhibhoutik, adhidaivik and adhyatmik problems associated within oneself, immediate, surrounding and far away).

Om Naham Karta Harih Karta
Tat Puja Karma chakilam
Tathapi Matkrita Puja
Tat Prasadena Nanyatha
Om Shantihi Shantihi Shantihi

ॐ नाहं कर्ता हरिः कर्ता तत्पूजा कर्म चाखिलम् ।
तथापि मत्कृता पूजा तत्प्रसादेन नान्यथा ।।
ॐ शान्तिः शान्तिः शान्तिः

I am not the doer, Hari is the doer. Still, I got this opportunity to do the Puja by Hari's Grace alone.

Om Lord is Jnanananda Swarupa (Knowledge-bliss personified). Om Lord is Jnanananda Swarupa (Knowledge-bliss personified).
