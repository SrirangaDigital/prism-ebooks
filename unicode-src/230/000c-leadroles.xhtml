Lead Roles

Rama, pronounced as ‘RAAMA,’ Prince of Ayodhya.

Sita, pronounced as SEETA, Rama’s wife

Rama’s brothers are Lakshmana, Bharata and Shatrugna

Hanuman, pronounced as ‘HANUMAAN, ’the Vanara hero devoted to Rama.

‘Vanara’ is Pronounced as ‘Vaanara.’

Rama’s other friend is Sugreeva, the Vanara King of Kishkinda.

Ravana, pronounced as ‘RAAVANA, the Lord of Lanka.

He is described symbolically as having ten heads having strength and intelligence of ten men.
