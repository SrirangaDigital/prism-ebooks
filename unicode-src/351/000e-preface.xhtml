Gratitude

‘The deepest craving of human nature is the need to be appreciated.’

William James

However, in my journey with each one of you, I saw beyond this craving, a genuine ‘giving’ filled with generosity. I have no words to express what I experienced. Hence I am quoting the words of some well-known people to express my gratitude.

‘No one who achieves success does so without the help of others.’

Alfred North Whitehead

I acknowledge the contribution of each one of you with gratitude:

‘Gratitude is when memory is stored in the heart and not in the mind.’

Lionel Hampton

Each one of you has engraved yourself in my heart and mind.

‘I would maintain that thanks are the highest form of thought, and that gratitude is happiness doubled by wonder.’

Gilbert C. Chesterton

I feel wonderful with all of you around and with me, in this journey of becoming an author. I have made new friends and amazing relationships have evolved and I have been able to see the hidden talents of many. These relationships have been the force that made me get up and write when I was stuck or when I questioned myself - Should I even do this?’

‘Gratitude unlocks the fullness of life. It turns what we have into enough, and more. It turns denial into acceptance, chaos to order, confusion to clarity. It can turn a meal into a feast, a house into a home, a stranger into a friend.’

Melody Beattie
