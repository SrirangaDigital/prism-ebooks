Rock Climbing and Mountaineering gear / Equipment

*United Institute of Alpine Association (UIAA) is the certifying authority of mountaineering equipment .

1. Nylon Rope: Used for climbing. There are two kinds: dynamic rope and the static rope. Usually, a dynamic rope is used for climbing. Ropes of varying diameters are available. A 10.5 mm rope is mostly used. A rope should have a minimum of 1500 kg breaking strength. Each rope has its own breaking strength. The ‘number of falls’ count for the usage. Some ropes go useless after 7 and some after 12 falls.

2. Seat Harness: It is worn like a trouser. All the equipment that has to be attached to a mountaineer is connected to the Seat Harness with the help of a Carabiner.

3. Carabiner: The Carabiner works as a linking device to the climber and his climbing equipment. It acts as a bridge between the rope and the self. Three kinds of Carabiners are used – Screw, Plane and Autolock. The breaking strength of a carabiner varies between 25kN when locked and 8 kN when unlocked.

4. Helmet

5. Mittens

6. Jumar - Also called Ascender and Descender. Used for jumaring. One of the most important equipment for scaling the Everest. Helps in climbing up and down.

7. Sling: It connects the carabiner and other equipment. It can sustain weights up to 2200 kg. Two types of slings are used: Tubular sling is used for rescue operations and Tape sling for extensions.

8. Ladder: Usually portable and foldable.

9. Rock piton: The four kinds of pitons used are Vertical, Horizontal, Angular and Multipurpose. Rock piton is used while climbing rocks. Ice piton or tubular piton is used in snow and ice or glaciers. The ice piton is driven into ice in clockwise and anti clockwise directions. It is made of titanium.

10. Chockmates: Are used to gain a grip between cracks and have 1000 kg breaking point.

11. Belay device / Verso: used while climbing. Belay device is connected to the harness of the carabiner. The belayer holds the belaying device. He controls the movement of the climber.

12. Pulley: Pulleys of different breaking strength are used. For instance, 11 kN on side and to 22 kN on both sides. (1kN or Kilo Newton equals to 101.97 Kilogram force unit.)

13. Expansion bolt: helps in making a permanent anchor. The expansion bolt when hammered into the rock opens up like a flower, thus increasing the grip for the anchor. These bolts cannot be removed once inserted into the rock.

14. Chalk Bag

15. Shoes

16. SLCD: Spring Loaded Caming Device also called Friends. The caming action is caused because of the spring. These are used to find a grip between cracks of huge rocks or mountains.
