About the Second Edition

Knowledge is fathomless. Every yoga class is a new learning for me. All the students are my teachers and their sincerity and commitment takes me deeper into yoga. This will be a ready reference book for all sincere seekers of yoga.

More kriyas, asanas and mudras have been added in this edition. The newly added section on puja mudra will be beneficial during the practitioner’s daily ritualistic worship.

As an easy reference for the users of this book (including practitioners and teachers), all the asanas, kriyas, mudras, pranayamas and meditation techniques have been categorized into five levels and provided in a pictorial form in Appendix A. Some YouTube references are given in Appendix B. Yoga class prayers are given in Appendix C.

I would like to thank Edelweiss Symons, Anila Kennedy, Diptiman Yadav and Sumana Rajendra for editing this book, Amrut Rajendra for taking photos and videos; and acknowledge S-VYASA’s Bharatheesha P for the design and layout.

This book is dedicated to the indweller of all — Lord Vasudeva. May He illumine our paths.
