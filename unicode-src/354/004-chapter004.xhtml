Hata Yoga Pradipika and Gheranda Samhita

Both Hata Yoga Pradipika and Gheranada Samhita heal with asana, pranayama and mudras in detail. Swami Swatmaram in the 16th century compiled his famout treatise, Hata Yoga Pradipika, in four chapters and 389 verses.

The Gheranda Samhita is a discussion between the sage Gheranda and his disciple Chandakaapaali, divided into seven chapters and cpmprisin 338 verses. It closely follows in the footsteps of Hata Yoga Pradipika, which teaches Yoga through seven practises or Sadhanas:

  1. Purification: Through Shat Karma Kriya
  2. Strengthening: Through Asana
  3. Steadying: Through Mudra
  4. Calming : Through Pratyahara
  5. lightening : Through Pranayama
  6. Perception : Through Dhyana
  7. Isolation : Through Samadhi, Freedom.
